About Me

I am an ordinary individual with a deep passion for IT since 2018. In 2019, I ventured into the cyber underground community, immersing myself in the complexities of cybersecurity. It was during this time that I adopted the alias "Psychoh4x0r," inspired by the name "h4x0r1337." My journey in the underground world intensified throughout 2019 and 2020, where I honed my skills and collaborated with various groups.

My first affiliation was with Jakarta Blackhat, a pivotal step in my early exploration of the underground scene. However, by November of that year, I decided to leave and, in 2021, joined FoursDeath Team. In March of the same year, I became part of AnonSec Team, further expanding my expertise. As my experience grew, I founded my own team, LulzGhost Team, and later became a member of Manusia Biasa Team, a well-established group known for its long-standing presence in the underground community since 2012.

By November 2022, I chose to step away, marking a temporary hiatus from the underground world. However, in early January 2025, I returned, reigniting my journey in cybersecurity and ethical hacking. My path has been shaped by years of hands-on experience, collaboration with top-tier teams, and a relentless pursuit of knowledge in the ever-evolving landscape of cybersecurity.
